Everything you need to ship faster

DevStream is built around the workflow of modern frontend teams — record, review, iterate, and share without friction.

Dual-view Recording

Desktop and mobile captured side-by-side in a single video. No stitching, no post-processing — what you see is what you share.

Percentage Scroll Sync

Scroll position is tracked as a ratio (0–1) so both views stay aligned even when their page heights differ. No oscillation, no drift.

10 Device Presets

iPhone SE through 15 Pro, Galaxy S24 Ultra, Pixel 8, OnePlus 12 — all with accurate viewports, notches, dynamic islands, and bezel styles.

Cloud Storage

Recordings upload to Supabase automatically after you stop. Access and manage all your sessions from the web dashboard.

Shareable Links

One-click share URL per recording. Recipients can watch without signing in — perfect for async reviews with designers and clients.

Privacy First

Row-level security via Supabase RLS ensures your recordings are only accessible to you — unless you choose to share a link.

Up and running in 60 seconds

No configuration. No accounts to set up first. Install and start recording immediately.
